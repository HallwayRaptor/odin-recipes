Yogurt Parfait

A simple and cool treat

This recipe is for an american style layered fruit and yogurt parfait. I'm using frozen blueberries, plain greek yogurt, 85% chocolate, topped with walnuts. You can substitute any ingredients you like.

Ingredients

  • 2% greek yogurt
  • fresh or thawed blueberries
  • 85% dark chocolate
  • blueberry jam
  • crushed walnuts

Steps

  1. Add yogurt to a dish
  2. spread jam over yogurt
  3. add berries
  4. melt chocolate and pour on top. Shake to coat evenly
  5. top with walnuts
  6. cover and refrigerate for one hour
