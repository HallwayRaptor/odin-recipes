French Omlette

A Classic French Recipe

This is a recipe for a traditional French omelette. A traditional French omelette uses only a few simple ingredients to create a delicious lunch dish with no frills but lots of flavour.

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 1 tbs butter
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 1 pinch of black pepper

Steps

  1. Heat butter in a non-stick pan over medium heat. Do not brown the butter
  2. While butter is heating vigorously mix eggs, salt, and pepper with a fork or tiny whisk
  3. Pour the eggs into the pan
  4. Vigourously stir the eggs with a small silicone spatula or fork. If you're using a fork be careful not to scrape the pan with the tongs
  5. Constantly shake the pan with your other hand while stirring the eggs to evenly distribute the eggs and prevent clumping
  6. When the eggs stop filling in the scraped areas reduce heat and smooth out with the spatula
  7. Once the eggs are no longer liquid on top carefully fold the edge closest to the handle on top of itself, about 1 inch in.
  8. Continue flipping until you are about 2 inches from the end. Then flip the other side on top
  9. Without flipping the egg, melt some butter under the omelette for about 3 seconds
  10. Pick up the pan and, using the spatula or fork, flip the omelette over on to your plate so the crease is underneath
  11. (Optional) Garnish with paprika and chives
