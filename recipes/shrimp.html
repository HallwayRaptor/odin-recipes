Shimp Cocktail

Shrimp With Cocktail Sauce

This is a basic shrimp cocktail recipe that you can make with simple ingredients at home.

Ingredients

Shrimp

  • uncooked shrimp
  • peppercorns
  • salt
  • garlic
  • lemons
  • bay leaf
  • parsley

Sauce

  • ketchup
  • worcestershire sauce
  • lemon
  • hot sauce
  • horseradish

Steps

  1. Peel and devein the shrimp
  2. add peppercorns, garlic, salt, parsley, lemon juice and bay leaf to boiling water
  3. add shrimp and boil for 2 minutes then put in ice bath to cool
  4. while shrimp is cooling, whisk together all sauce ingredients
